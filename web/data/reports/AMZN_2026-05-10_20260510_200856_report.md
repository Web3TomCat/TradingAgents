## Key Fundamental Risks
1. **AI Infrastructure Overbuild Risk:** Amazon is spending ~$130B+/year on CapEx. If AI demand growth decelerates, these assets could under-earn their cost of capital. (Risk: Medium-High)
2. **Regulatory/Antitrust Risk:** FTC lawsuit (filed 2023), potential EU Digital Markets Act pressure, potential forced separation of AWS or marketplace. (Risk: Medium)
3. **AWS Competition:** Microsoft Azure (with OpenAI partnership) and Google Cloud are both gaining share in AI workloads. AWS dominance is not guaranteed. (Risk: Medium)
4. **Retail Margin Compression:** If consumer spending slows (recession), lower-margin retail becomes a larger revenue share, pressuring overall margins. (Risk: Low-Medium)
5. **SBC Overhang:** Stock-based compensation remains significant (~$19.5B/year), creating ongoing dilution. (Risk: Low-Medium)
6. **Currency / International Macro:** ~40%+ of revenue is international, exposed to FX volatility and weaker consumer economies. (Risk: Low)
